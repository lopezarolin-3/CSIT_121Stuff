VESPERA

AGE: 21

Element: Ice and Shadow

The night grows colder when Vespera is near.

Vespera bends ice and shadows to her will, freezing the air and vanishing into darkness with ease. She can summon a pack of ghostly wolves that move silently through the night, striking without warning. Calm and mysterious, she is both a shadow in the cold and a storm no one sees coming.
