KENJI

AGE: 16

Element: Smoke

Summon: Smoke Cat

Weapon: Katana

Moves like drifting smoke, silent, and impossible to hold.

Kenji's long silver-white hair flows behind him in sharp arcs, mirroring the sweep of his blade. His crimson eyes glow with a focused intensity, giving him a predatory, calculating presence. His expression is cold and composed, as if every movement has already been decided before it begins.
