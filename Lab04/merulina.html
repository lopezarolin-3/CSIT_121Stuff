MERULINA

AGE: 18

Element: Water

The ocean gets angry, it's her you should fear.

Merulina has the power to control water in any form. She can lift waves, twist rivers, and make rain fall whenever she wants. She can also call forth a sea dragon from the depths to fight by her side. Quiet and strong, she holds the power of the sea within her.
