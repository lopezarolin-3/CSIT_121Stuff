Advantages of PNG

Home How It Works Advantages Sources

High Quality

No quality loss after saving multiple times. Perfect to use for graphics, screenshots and diagrams.

Transparency Support

Great for logos and web design elements. You can place images on any background cleanly with sharp edges and solid colors.

Example for Backgrounds

body {
  background-image: url("background.png");
}

This could be incorporated in your CSS style file.

Displaying a PNG Image

<img src="image.png" alt="Example PNG">

When to Use and How

Use PNG when quality and transparency is more important than the file size. Also when your builkding a website and you want a crisp visual, PNG is the safer option..
